Jazz Student Gigs

Jazz Student Gigs is an independent, volunteer run contact point where community members can share performance opportunities with student jazz musicians.

We do not book gigs, employ musicians, or manage performance arrangements. Messages sent to the coordinator are forwarded to interested families, who handle all decisions, communication, scheduling, and payments independently.
